## 3. Visual System To Borrow From IPSC Example

Use:
- White or very light cards for explanation slides
- Purple/teal accent border only, not full dark cinematic everywhere
- Big icon + short label per point
- One story per slide
- Customer journey maps for before/after
- Big number slide for opportunity
- Business Model Canvas only if simplified; avoid tiny text

Avoid:
- Dense technical diagrams on every slide
- Too many glowing boxes
- Claiming Last Gasp/SCADA/LINE text mining as production-ready unless owner approval exists
- Saying realized saving, paid customer, or Auto ETR live

Recommended palette:
- Background: white / very pale cyan for body slides
- Accent: PEA purple, electric teal, small amber risk color
- Text: dark navy
- Guardrail chip: gray/navy with `mode = shadow | production_send = blocked`
